More About Us

Debachanya Girl Child Empowerment Initiative is a foundation whose aim is to help vulnerable population especially girls have basic, necessary access to education and source of livelihood.

Our Mission

Our mission is to mobilize non-school going girls, between ages 6 and 17 from economically and socially backward rural areas in Nigeria and put them firmly in track of literacy through quality primary and secondary education. This mission supports the acceleration and the attainment of the 1 - 6 and 16 SDGs related to ending poverty and hunger, ensuring healthy lives for all, ensuring inclusive and quality education, integrating water hygeine and sanitation and to promote peace and inclusive society for sustainable development.
